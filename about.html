Teaching Experience

Tom Quinn has more than ten years of classroom teaching experience in public, private, and parochial schools in New York and Connecticut.

He has taught grades 6 through 12 including mathematics, social studies, science, and computer programming.

Tom also served as an Adjunct Professor of Computer Programming at Baruch College and the College of Insurance in New York City.

His teaching philosophy emphasizes clear explanations, step‑by‑step instruction, and building strong analytical problem‑solving skills.

Today he provides personalized math tutoring sessions online via Zoom, helping students nationwide strengthen their understanding of mathematics.
