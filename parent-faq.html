Frequently Asked Questions

How are sessions conducted?

All tutoring sessions are conducted live via Zoom. Students receive one-on-one instruction with explanations, guided practice, and real-time feedback.

What subjects do you cover?

Tutoring is available for Algebra, Geometry, SAT Math, and general middle school mathematics.

How long is each tutoring session?

Sessions may be scheduled as single meetings or as part of a multi-session package, depending on the student’s needs.

Do students need any special software?

Students only need Zoom and a reliable internet connection. A notebook, calculator, and class materials are also helpful.

How do payments work?

Payments are made online in advance through PayPal using the payment page on this website.

Who is online tutoring best for?

Online tutoring works well for students who benefit from individualized attention, structured explanations, and flexible scheduling.
